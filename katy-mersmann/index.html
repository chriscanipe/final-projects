Where Lead is Released in Missouri

By Katy Mersmann
Published May 5, 2016

Factories or other industrial facilities releasing lead, and other chemicals, in the United States must report what chemical compounds they're releasing, and the amounts, to the Environmental Protection Agency. The EPA collects that data and releases it in a Toxic Release Index each year. In 2014, at least 88 facilities in Missouri reported that they released some lead into the air, soil or water on the property. Facilities with "zero" listed reported a lead release under a low threshold set by the EPA.
